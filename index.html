Frequently asked questions

Are these tools really free?

Yes — every tool on Lekkal is free to use, with no signup, no watermark on outputs, and no usage limits. We may add a paid tier in the future with extra features (auto-save, team workspaces, API access), but the core tools will remain free.

Does Lekkal store my data?

No. All Lekkal tools run entirely in your browser. Your invoice data, calculator inputs, and other information never leave your device. Saved drafts are stored only in your browser's local storage, which you control.

What does "Lekkal" mean?

Lekkal (లెక్క) is a Telugu word meaning ledger, accounts, or bookkeeping. We chose it because it captures what these tools are for — keeping the books, running the numbers, and making the back office painless.

Can I use these tools commercially?

Absolutely. The tools are designed for founders, freelancers, agencies, and small businesses. Invoices generated by Lekkal are commercially valid and contain no Lekkal branding or watermarks — they're yours to send to clients as-is.

Will you add more tools?

Yes — expense splitter and HSN code lookup are next on the roadmap. We've shipped GST, EMI, burn rate / runway, and ROI calculators alongside the invoice generator. If there's a specific tool you'd find useful, drop us a note on GitHub.
